Amrita Vishwa Vidhyapeetham

The university was founded with the opening of Amrita School of Engineering, Coimbatore, in 1994 by Mata Amritanandamayi Devi and is managed by her international humanitarian organization, Mata Amritanandamayi Math. In 2003, it became one of the youngest institutes to be accredited as a deemed-to-be university by the UGC. In 2002, campuses were also established in Amritapuri and Bangalore.

TIFAC-CORE in Cyber Security

Established in 2005 at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore, the institution is recognized for its collaborative approach between industry and academia. It offers M. Tech and Ph.D. programs in Cyber Security, with a focus on cutting-edge research and educational initiatives aimed at addressing current and future security challenges. Through its innovative curriculum and partnership model, the CORE aims to develop world-class expertise in Cyber Security.

ISAC

ISAC (Information Sharing and Analysis Center) is an organization that facilitates the exchange of cybersecurity information among its members, typically within a specific industry or sector. ISAC Foundation is a non-profit entity that supports the development and operation of ISACs worldwide, providing resources, guidance, and collaboration opportunities to strengthen cybersecurity information sharing efforts.

COP-CONNECT

The CopConnect National Security Database is a comprehensive database designed to provide law enforcement agencies with access to critical information related to national security threats. It serves as a central repository for data, intelligence, and resources that can assist law enforcement personnel in identifying, preventing, and responding to potential security risks.
